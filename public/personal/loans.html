How do I qualify?

Most loans are only available to Kansas City area borrowers or existing nbkc customers.

Check the description of the loan you’re interested in below before you apply.

Local counties we serve:

Kansas:

Douglas, Johnson, Leavenworth, Miami, Shawnee and Wyandotte.

Missouri:

Buchanan, Cass, Clay, Jackson, Platte, and Ray.

Other specific qualification requirements vary by product and loan type. To see if you qualify, contact the loan professional listed for each product below.

Loans we offer


Home equity loans

Own a home? Use your investment to get the funding you need now. Borrow against your existing home equity to finance a renovation, start a project, or whatever else you need to finance.

Currently only lending to borrowers in the Kansas City area.

Apply here

Automobiles. Motorcycles. Boats. RVs.

Get where you wanna go with an auto loan. Use it to purchase your next big toy — whether it’s a car, motorcycle, boat, or RV. Fuzzy dice not included.

Currently only lending to customers in the Kansas City area.

Apply here for automobilesApply here for motorcycles, boats, & RVs

CD secured loans

Got a Certificate of Deposit (CD) account with nbkc? A CD secured loan can get you the funds you need fast.

Available to nbkc CD account holders only.

Apply here

Bridge loans and real estate lot purchase loans

If you haven’t sold your house and are looking to buy a new one, a bridge loan can help you finance it. Or if you’ve got your eye on a great piece of land, a real estate lot purchase loan can make it yours until you’re ready to build.

Currently only available to borrowers in the Kansas City area and non-local borrowers with an existing nbkc home loan or mortgage.

Apply here

Home construction

Own a lot of land and need help building your dream house? A home construction loan may be an option.

This loan only applies to brand new construction projects.

Borrowers should come prepared with an appraisal for the completed home, title work, a cost budget analysis and a plan.

Currently only lending to customers in the Kansas City area.

Apply here
