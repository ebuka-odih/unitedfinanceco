Good Things to Know

Rate updates

Interest rates and APY may change after account opening.

Hold on deposits

Deposits made within the first 30 days of opening a new account will be held for 5 business days before the funds are made available, and possibly up to 9 business days depending on the situation. Additional holds on deposits may occur even after the initial 30-day period, but funds are typically available the next business day.
