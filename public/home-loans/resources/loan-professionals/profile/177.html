Advice for new home buyers

Make sure that you work with some that you connect with and that responds to your questions in a reasonable time. Test to see if they respond on evenings and weekends. Also, be sure that you are looking at more than just the rate. Many lenders will show you a low rate but are charging you extra fees to get to that rate.
